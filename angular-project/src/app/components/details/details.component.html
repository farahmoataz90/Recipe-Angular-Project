Ingredients:
  • . Stuffing: Rice, onion, olive oil, tomato paste, pine nuts (optional), parsley, dill (optional), allspice, cinnamon, salt, pepper, lemon juice, pomegranate molasses (optional).
  • . Grape Leaves: Jar of grape leaves (rinsed and drained).
  • . Cooking: Water or vegetable broth, olive oil, extra lemon juice (optional).
Cooking Steps:

1. Prepare the stuffing: Saut√© onion in olive oil, add rice, spices, and other ingredients. Simmer until rice is slightly undercooked, then let cool.
2. Prepare grape leaves: Rinse and trim grape leaves.
3. Stuff the leaves: Place rice mixture on each leaf, fold, and roll tightly.
4. Cook: Stack rolls in a pot, add water/broth, and cook for 45-60 minutes until tender.
5. Serve: Garnish with lemon juice or pomegranate molasses.
